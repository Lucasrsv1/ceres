Try the Cherry Tomato. Tomatoes slightly smaller than the conventional and with a sweeter flavor. How to Perform Planting Make the earth very soft and make small holes with your finger. Insert the seeds and sprinkle the soil on top. Until the seedlings are born and are small, water once a day in the morning. When the tomato is firmer, water three times a week. The tomato stem does not support its own weight, put a stake in the ground and, as it grows, tie it to support it. Cherry tomatoes should receive at least 4 hours of sunshine daily.
